My start to blender

I got to know about blender through a YouTube recommendation. Since then, I've never looked back. As any beginner blender artist does, I started with Andrew Price's Donut tutorial series. The result was not that great- But I just started. A month later, after a few more tutorials and experimenting, I re-did the donut. It was so much better. I learned a lot in that time and it made me really happy.

My First Attempt:
- Didn't understand most of the things; Lighting, a little bit of modelling etc.

My second attempt a month later:
- Much better modelling
- I did some sculpting and texture painting.
- Added a simple background to divert the attention to the donut.
